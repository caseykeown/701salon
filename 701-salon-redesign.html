The 701 point of view

Come for the work.
Stay for the energy.

701 is a collective of independent artists who each run their own book and bring their own specialty. Curly cutting, blonding, extensions, color correction, nails, tattoo work, permanent makeup, lashes, and brows all live here.

It feels polished without feeling precious. Plants in every corner. Cold drinks on the shelf. Good music. Honest conversation. A room built for artists to do their best work and for clients to feel completely at ease.

Owner + stylist

Beka Ballard

Built for artists.

Beka opened 701 in 2021 with a simple idea: create the kind of salon where talented people could build their own business, make their best work, and still feel like part of something bigger.

The result is a space with its own personality. Clients get greeted by name. Artists get room to create. Nobody has to fit into somebody else’s mold.
