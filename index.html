OUR STORY

A Little Bohemian,
A Lot of Heart

701 Salon & Spa isn't one chair and one point of view. It's a house of independent artists who chose to work under the same roof, each running their own book, each bringing a specialty the others don't have. Curly cutting. Blonding. Extensions. Color correction. Nails. Tattoo and permanent makeup next door at 701 Studio.

What ties it together is the room itself: plants in every corner, snacks and cold drinks on the shelf, seating you'd actually want to sit in, and a crew that our clients describe as down to earth and genuinely fun. Come once and it stops feeling like an appointment.

THE OWNER

Beka Ballard

Beka opened 701 in 2021 with a specific idea in mind: build the kind of salon where talented people could do their best work without being managed into someone else's mold, and where clients get greeted by name the second they walk through the door.

Clients have followed her for years, and the reviews keep landing on the same two words: her work, and her welcome. Both are the reason this place feels the way it does.
