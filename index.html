OUR STORY

A Little Bohemian,
A Lot of Heart

Tucked into the heart of Elizabethtown, 701 Salon & Spa was built around a simple idea: getting your hair or skin done should feel like time well spent, not a task to rush through. Our space blends warm textures, soft light, and organic touches with the precision and artistry of a truly professional team.

From lived-in balayage to relaxing facials, every service is approached with the same intention: listen closely, work carefully, and send you out feeling like the best version of yourself.
